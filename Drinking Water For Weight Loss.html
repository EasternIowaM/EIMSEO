Drinking Water For Weight Loss

When it come to losing weight, most people focus on the foods they eat. This is important, drinking water for weight loss may be more important than even food.Even if you just have a little belly fat that you are trying to get rid of, it may be a lot easier to lose if you were to just drink more water!Most people don't drink enough water and most people are also toting around a little bit of extra weight. Is this just a coincidence? Not at all. Not drinking enough water is probably the exact reason that those last few pounds to lose are always the most stubborn.One of the jobs that your liver performs, is to convert fat into energy. It also helps out the kidneys when they aren't able to work properly. Your kidneys need lots and lots of water to work their best. When you don't drink enough water the kidneys don't function properly so the liver has to help them out. This takes away from the livers other duties and results in excess stored fat. When you get enough water, you will begin to lose weight.To find out how much water your body needs, it helps to have a drinking water for weight loss calculator or be able to factor the amounts in your mind. To calculate your own need for water to begin losing more weight, multiply your weight by 0.7, then add 12 for each half hour of activity. This results in the number of ounces of water you need to drink to excellerate weight loss.
